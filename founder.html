Meet the Founder

The story behind CodeCom and the vision driving us forward.

SY

Stanley Yamoah

// Founder & Lead Developer

Hi, I'm Stanley Yamoah — the founder of CodeCom.

I started CodeCom with one goal: to make quality website design accessible and affordable for businesses, nonprofits, and individuals.

As a self-taught web developer from Ghana, I've worked with clients across different industries and learned that many great ideas and organizations struggle simply because they don't have a strong online presence. That's where CodeCom comes in.

Through this agency, I've been able to focus on what I'm passionate about: building impactful websites and software that help businesses grow and communities thrive.

I believe in transparency, creativity, and the power of the internet to transform lives and communities. Whether you're looking to go digital or scale your existing presence — you're welcome here.

Let's build something amazing, together.

— Stanley Yamoah
