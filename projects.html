SmartSchool

A comprehensive school management system designed to digitize and streamline educational operations. SmartSchool handles everything from student enrollment and attendance tracking to grade management, fee processing, and parent communication.

Built for schools that want to move beyond paper-based systems, SmartSchool provides an intuitive dashboard for administrators, teachers, students, and parents — all in one unified platform. Real-time reporting and analytics help school leaders make data-driven decisions.

CampusMart

A university-focused marketplace where students can buy, sell, and book services within their campus community. Students can sell textbooks, electronics, and other items, or offer services like tutoring, delivery, and design.

The platform uses online payment systems for secure payments and a unique confirmation code system to ensure safe transactions. Built specifically for campus life, it connects students at the same university while providing sellers with tools to manage listings, track earnings, and grow their business.
