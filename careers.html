Product Sales Representative — SmartSchool

Company: SmartSchool | Location: Ghana | Type: Full-Time

About SmartSchool:
SmartSchool is a modern school management system designed to help schools manage academics, administration, communication, and records efficiently. We are expanding and looking for motivated individuals to join our sales team.

Job Summary:
We are seeking confident and results-driven Sales Representatives to promote and sell the SmartSchool management system to school administrators. The ideal candidate should be persuasive, professional, and passionate about helping schools adopt technology solutions.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Promote and sell the SmartSchool system to schools and educational institutions
  • Meet with school administrators to present product features and benefits
  • Generate leads and follow up with potential clients
  • Achieve assigned sales targets
  • Build and maintain strong relationships with clients
  • Provide feedback from the market to improve sales strategies

Requirements:

  • Must be 18 years or older
  • Must possess a valid Ghana Card
  • Strong communication and persuasion skills
  • Confident, self-motivated, and goal-oriented
  • Previous sales experience is an advantage (but not mandatory)

Compensation: GHS 1,500 monthly salary
